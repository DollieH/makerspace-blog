What is a Makerspace?

A Makerspace is a place for students to engage in hands-on learning and creative problem solving, combining elements of science, technology, engineering, art, and math (STEAM). Itâ€™s a place for students to build, tinker, craft, and solve problems in creative ways and develop a growth mindset.
